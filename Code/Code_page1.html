Food: A Cultural Experience

Traveling through various places in the world it is hard to avoid the traditional cultural foods of the region. Most people enjoy tasting the culture. It’s different and interesting.


So let’s talk about food.


CHINA

In the southern region of China, the city of Guangzhou sits on Zhujiang River, which is home to Canton Tower.



  • One of three of China’s largest cities.
  • It serves a major role in modern commerce and is notable for its annual Canton Fair, China’s oldest and largest trade fair.
  • The streets are filled with fruitful markets and fascinating street cuisine.
  • Food is intensely flavorful and spicy, with a unique numbing agent.


    • Image. Gif. 2018. A river fish slathered in cilantro, mushroom, beansprouts and other vegtables stewed in a numbing red sauce. Served with white rice on the side

INDIA

India, a cultural soup reflected in there cooking. Region defines the foods that the people eat.

The Himilayan Region of Himachal Pradesh




  • The people of the mountain have the freshest food.
  • Their water is sourced directly from the glaciers of the Himilayan mountains.
  • The food is simple yet full of bold flavors.


    • Image. 2018. Very simple bean curry with white rice all grown on the side of the mountain around the village.

Thailand, a cultural hub of touristic splender.
